# Ruihuan Cheng

I am a Research Associate in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at The University of Hong Kong (HKU).

My research focuses on understanding heat transport in crystalline materials through first-principles calculations, molecular dynamics simulations, and machine-learning interatomic potentials. My work combines lattice dynamics, anharmonic phonon theory, and data-driven approaches to uncover the microscopic origins of ultralow thermal conductivity and anomalous thermal transport.

## Research Interests

* Lattice dynamics and phonon transport
* Strongly anharmonic materials
* Machine-learning interatomic potentials
* Ultralow thermal conductivity materials
* Dynamic disorder and superionic conductors
* AI for materials discovery
